Age group 12 - 17

Preparation

Classes of this age group usually have a duration of 45 minutes and 1:30h. The 45 min classes are usually taught in state school, which means that you will be traveling around 1:30h to get to the school. The 1:30h classes are classes that are taught at one of the new world centers. These are topic classes which means that you will be preparing a topic to teach in the class. In the 1:30 h classes we use course material from english books e.g. Family and Friends 2-6 (author Naomi Simmons). And in the 45 min classes we use course material from the english books Tieng Anh (4-8) by authors Bo Giao Duc and Va Dao Tao.

Warm-up

Classes normally start with introducing the kids to basic questions like for example how are you? what's your name? how many people are in your family? who is the youngest? Etc (see list). In the 1:30 h classes more questions can be asked like: how was your week, weekend etc?. Some classes also enjoy singing a songs, we have created a list of songs you can sing with the kids, note however that not all of the classes enjoy singing songs, you could ask before hand if the kids are in the mood for singing a song. Some Volunteers also bring music instruments like guitars to class (link).

Teaching

The main part of the class is based on the lesson plan that the Vietnamese teachers at New World whom base the plan on the contents of the english we books we use in the state schools. Lesson plans just present the topic and some tips on how to teach the topic however, you can choose yourself on how you are going to teach the topic. The 1:30 classes are topic classes, in these classes you can choose what to teach. There are many online sources you can use for inspiration on which topic to teach and how you can teach (see list of online teaching sources).

Games

Usually the classes are finalized with a game. We have prepared a list of games you can utilize in the classes. Have a read.
