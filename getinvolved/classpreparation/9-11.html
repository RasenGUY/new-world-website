Age group 9 - 11

Preparation

The majority classes pertaining to these age groups usually have a duration of around 45 min and are mostly taught in state schools. Even Though, the format is more or less the same as in classes taught in the other age groups. The content taught is different.

Warm-up

Normally, you can do some exercise with the students. But in some cases the students will not be responsive to it. You can then just proceed with the class, by for example introducing the students to different ways of greeting, or any of the topic /questions in the list provided by our volunteers. It is always good to implement and interactive startup by for example to using an object to throw to students to get them to stand up and participate. Furthermore, next to questions, you can also warm-up by doing some pronunciation exercises ex. Doing tongue twisters or singing songs.

Teaching

Usually you get a lesson plan from the vietnamese teachers based in the center. These lesson plans incorporate topics from the english books we use in the class. However, these plans aren’t always effective and therefore you have to do some additional planning on how you will direct the class. You can do this in many ways, as you chose to. Here are a few examples:

Role playing: you can write the word acting or roleplaying on the board and create circle around them. Then you can draw an arrow to the word situation which you have written its definition on the board before hand (you can also let the students themselves explain the words definition by going around and asking the students). Then you can ask the students “where can these situations happen”. You can then brainstorm with the students on different situations that they see everyday and create roleplaying game around a few of the situations that you have chosen. This of course depends on you time limit and the level of english the students are in. The bigger the situation the more students can get involved into the role playing.

Poems - sometimes you can use some of the vocabulary words presented in the english books. The words can be used in classes by for example creating stories, poems or songs using the words.

Word clouds - Use the same works from the book (see poems). You can split the class in two teams or three depending on the size of a the classroom. Chose an amount of words each team should finish. Write the words jumbled together with random letters in a cloud pertaining to each word. Each team will be given points depending on the amount of words they solved. The teams have to writing down all words in a specified amount of time.

Games

If there is any time left in your class you can end the class with a game. In many cases students look forward to finalizing the class with a game. We have created a list of all of the possible games that you can play with the students (see list)
