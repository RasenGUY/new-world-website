Volunteering

Volunteering in Thai Binh, Vietnam

General Information

So you are thinking about volunteering at the New World Center ? Here are a few things you should know. Our center is home to around 20 volunteers from all over the world. This is also the maximum amount of volunteers our center can accomodate at a time. Yes accomodate, if you decide to volunteer at our center you will be provided accommodation, food and compensation for your help. The compensation is a fixed amount of around 200 USD per month. It is important to note however, that we are not an NGO, we are a social enterprise and thus we pay tax and employ a staff of around 6-7 vietnamese. For more detailed information about New World please refer to the about page.

Our Coordinators here in Thai Binh are Ms. Hong & Ms. Mai. Ms.Hong her Husband and two sons live a short distance away in Kien Xuong. Ms.Mai lives nearby with her two daughters in Vu Thu. They are the most kind , generous and hospitable people. And love to support us when and where they can. Our teaching aids/coordinators are Huong, Ly and Lien. These ladies are always around for the Volunteers and are super helpful. They prepare our Meals, Schedules and assist us with queries in regards classes,games and the roster. The girls are local and always know the best places to eat and visit to hang out and chill!

Accomodation

In Thai Binh , we have 2 houses the old house and the new house (main centre). The old house has 8 bedrooms (each houses one individual), spread across 3 floors, it is mainly used for housing the volunteers. There is a Bathroom on every floor with showering facilities. The switch on the outside of each bathroom heats the water in advance. Within 30 mins you will have enough Hot water. The bedrooms are quite generous in size. A wardrobe, bed (pillows & duvet) and a desk live in the room. Each Bedroom has a full functioning balcony for relaxing,chillaxing or spruce up with some colors and flowers, totally up to you.

The new house is the main center and is the place where some of the english classess are held. classess are held during the day, in the evenings and on weekends.
