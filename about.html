Who We Are!

The New World International English center is an organization set up to benefit kids of all ages set in the northern part of Vietnam. Hidden in the city of Thai Binh district, New world tries its best to support Vietnamese kids with learning the english language. It achieves this through the application of an array of games, workshops and lessons, with the help from a medley of foreign volunteers. New World sets itself apart from other english teaching centers in Vietnam in that it houses the largest amount of foreign english speaking volunteers in Thai Binh city.
